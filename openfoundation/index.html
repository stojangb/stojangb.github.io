Open Foundation

Open Foundation is a new project from StojanGasic.com. Our goal is to create an organization that drives all kinds of projects: from solidarity activities and support for people, to creative initiatives that test unconventional ideas.

Lines of action

  • Exercise machines that reward your effort with food.
  • City cleanup campaigns gathering volunteers and providing materials.
  • Development of ethical open-source and AI tools to detect corruption, improve access to justice and simplify municipal processes.
  • Connecting people and causes to highlight local organizations that need help.

All these projects will launch once we gather enough financial support.

Want to participate?

If you wish to donate or collaborate, send an email to stojangasic@gmail.com explaining why you'd like to join this initiative.
